About

We are an American home goods company founded in 2025.

The Craftiva collection is our line of daily-use objects for the home — honest materials, thoughtful design, built to last.

Founded on opposite coasts — Alexandria, Virginia and Sacramento, California — and united by the same frustration: most home goods are either commodity items or artisan pieces too precious to use. We're building the middle ground.

Our Standard

Material Honesty

We use solid wood. If it looks like oak, it is oak. No veneers. No engineered substitutes dressed up to look like something better.

Designed for Use

Every product is designed for the thing it will actually do. Not to look good in a photograph. Not to impress once. To be used every day and feel right every time.

Earned Place

A Craftiva object should earn its place in your home. That means it works well, feels right in the hand, and looks like it belongs — not like it was purchased to fill a space.
